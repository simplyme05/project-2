About The College Hangover

On a fateful hangover day, a group of college kids were struggling to find the perfect hangover restaurant, while in their drunken state one of them said: “screw this let’s make our own damn restaurant!” While at first, it was just a joke they all agreed that starting their own restaurant would be fun as hell!!

It took them 2 years to get funds for it, in those two years they all worked hard and came up with different appetizers and meals. It was starting to move forward they bought the perfect place near campus that just so happened to be perfectly located near all the bars.

To this day the same group of college kids, now adults still run The Hangover and could not be more successful. They enjoy watching all the new college kids come in with the biggest hangovers of their lives!
